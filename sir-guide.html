SIR 2026 GUIDE

Maharashtra SIR 2026: Aapka Voter Category Kaunsa Hai?

VoterCheck par aapke naam ki matching ke baad, hum aapko batate hain:

  • ✅ Aapka status kya hai (Legacy Voter, Current Only, Legacy Only, ya No Match)
  • 📝 Kaun sa form bharna hai (Form 6, Form 8, ya koi nahi)
  • 🎯 Next step kya hai (BLO enumeration, new registration, correction, etc.)
  • 📋 Kaun se documents tayyar karni hain

🔍 Pehla Step

VoterCheck par apna naam, DOB, aur parent/father name enter karo.

✅ Dusra Step

System aapko batadega: 2002 aur 2024 dono me nam mila ya nahi.

📋 Tisra Step

Match result ke hisaab se aapka category (Case 1, 2, 3, ya 4).

📝 Char Step

Form number aur documents ki complete guidance get karo.

Maharashtra SIR 2026: 4 Voter Categories & Form Guide

✅

Case 1: LEGACY VOTER (2002 ✓ + 2024 ✓)

Name exists in BOTH 2002 and 2024 rolls

Status: Verified & Confirmed

Form Required: No new form needed

Next Step: BLO enumeration verification (April onwards)

Documents to Keep: ID Proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID, PAN) + Address Proof

आपका नाम 2002 और 2024 दोनों विधानसभा सूचियों में मिल गया है। आप एक Legacy Voter हैं। BLO आपसे enumeration के समय मिलेंगे।

📝

Case 2: CURRENT ROLL ONLY (2024 ✓ + 2002 ✗)

Name in 2024 but NOT in 2002 roll

Status: Registered but needs mapping

Form Required: Form 6 (if no 2002 relative) OR provide relative details

Sub-Cases:

  • If parents/grandparents in 2002: Provide their name, father's name, DOB → Automatic mapping
  • If no relative in 2002: File Form 6 (New Voter Enrollment)

Documents to Keep: Birth Certificate, School Certificate, Aadhaar, Domicile Certificate (critical), Property/Rent Agreement

आप 2024 सूची में हैं लेकिन 2002 में नहीं। अगर आपके माता-पिता या दादा-दादी 2002 में थे तो उनका विवरण दें। नहीं तो Form 6 भरें।

⚠️

Case 3: LEGACY ONLY (2002 ✓ + 2024 ✗)

Name in 2002 but MISSING from 2024 roll

Status: Potentially deleted or not yet re-verified

Form Required: No form - SIR will restore

Why Missing? Could be: misspelling update, admin error, or deletion

Critical Action: During SIR enumeration (April onwards), be present when BLO visits. Confirm your continued eligibility.

If still not restored: File objection to ERO within 7 days of Draft Roll publication

Documents to Keep: ID Proof, Address Proof, Father's name, DOB confirmation

आप 2002 में थे लेकिन 2024 की सूची से हट गए हो। SIR प्रक्रिया के दौरान (अप्रैल से) BLO आपको verify करेंगे और restoration कर देंगे। अपना डेटा तैयार रखें।

❌

Case 4: NO MATCH (2002 ✗ + 2024 ✗)

Name NOT in either 2002 or 2024 rolls

Status: Not yet registered

Form Required: Form 6 (New Voter Registration)

Pre-Requisite: Must be 18+ years old on enrollment date

Critical Documents (12 sets required): Birth Certificate, School Certificate, Aadhaar, Domicile Certificate (to prove Maharashtra residency), Property/Rent agreement

Timeline: File Form 6 before Draft Roll is finalized (typically by May 2026)

Submission: Submit to BLO during door-to-door visit OR directly to ERO office

आप किसी भी सूची में नहीं हैं। आपको नया मतदाता रजिस्ट्रेशन (Form 6) करना होगा। महत्वपूर्ण: Domicile Certificate और Property Documents जमा करें।

Hamari taraf se di gayi jaankari ko form me kahan bhare?

Ham system se kya denge

  • Candidate Name Match Status (2002 / 2024)
  • AC code, Part number, probable roll year
  • Parent record match status
  • Premium Partner reference ID (campaign tracking)

User form me kahan dale

  • Old roll reference section me AC + part + year
  • Parent details section me father/mother name
  • Applicant detail section me self name/mobile
  • Declaration section me sign + date

Fraud Alert (OTP Safety)

SIR process ke naam par OTP maangne wale calls se bachein. Kisi unknown caller ko OTP, full Aadhaar number, bank details ya payment link par data share na karein.
